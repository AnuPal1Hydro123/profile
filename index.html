My Profile

About Me

I am a passionate hydrologist, water resources researcher, and flood modeler who enjoys tackling complex challenges related to river flow mechanisms, river hydraulics, sediment transport, channel geomorphology, and eco-hydraulic modeling. Beyond my research, I love listening to music, hiking, playing outdoor sports, and spending quality time with my family.

Awards: Jal Vigyan Purashkar (2023), awarded by Indian Society for Hydraulics
